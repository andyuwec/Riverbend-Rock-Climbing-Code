Rock Around the Bloc

This event is an annual holiday competition held at this gym with many different sponsors and levels of competition. This competition is one of the few in the world which actually sets competition Blocs by height, with ranges of 5'-5'5", 5'6"-5'11", and 6' and up. This takes into account height which can be a big factor in climbing

Boulder Beginnings

This event is an intro class on the wonderful world of bouldering, and how to do it. Bouldering is a discipline in climbing which does not use ropes or harnesses, and relies solely on the climber's ability to "just go up". This is a one session class which will happen once a week for a whole month, so pick a timeslot for yourself or your climber and join the fun.
