Our Mission

Our mission in the creation of this gym, was to create a space for the outdoor community of Eau Claire WI, especially in the winter months where there may not be that many easily accessible climbing spaces. This gym offers year round climbing of all different styles, classes on how to climb and climbing safely, and much more. Overall, our goal is to serve our community and create a welcoming space for years to come!

Our Story

Jaime Salamanca had always been captivated by the thrill of climbing. Growing up in the rolling hills of Vermont, he spent his childhood scrambling up rocks, climbing trees, and challenging himself to reach new heights. His passion for climbing only grew stronger as he aged, leading him to explore some of the most iconic climbing spots across the United States.

After graduating college with a degree in business management, Jaime worked in the corporate world for several years. However, the desk job left him yearning for the exhilaration he found on the cliffs. He knew he had to follow his passion and turn his lifelong hobby into something more.

In his early 30s, Jaime moved to Eau Claire, Wisconsin, a city with a burgeoning outdoor community but lacking a dedicated climbing gym. Seeing an opportunity to combine his business acumen with his love for climbing, he decided to take a leap of faith.

Jaime poured his heart, soul, and savings into opening "Riverbend Rock Climbing," Eau Claire's first indoor climbing facility. The gym featured state-of-the-art climbing walls, bouldering areas, and a dedicated space for training and yoga. He aimed to create a welcoming environment where climbers of all levels could connect, learn, and grow.

Riverbend quickly became a hub for local climbers and adventurers. Jaime's passion and dedication to the climbing community were evident in every aspect of the gym. He organized events, competitions, and workshops to foster a sense of camaraderie among climbers.

His efforts paid off as the gym flourished, attracting members from all over the region. Jaime's story inspired many to pursue their dreams, proving that with determination and a love for what you do, anything is possible. Riverbend Rock Climbing became more than just a gym; it became a testament to Jaime Salamanca's unyielding spirit and his love for climbing.
